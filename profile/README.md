## 🧠 About

PublicKernel is an open-source organization focused on building AI and data-driven systems that address real-world problems.

We develop scalable, transparent, and accessible solutions across machine learning, data analysis, and intelligent systems — with a strong emphasis on practical impact and open collaboration.
